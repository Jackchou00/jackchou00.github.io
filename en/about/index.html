About Me#

I’m Jack Chou.

I’m currently a graduate student in color science. For more about me and our work, please visit my personal page and lab website.

My hobbies include photography, cats, and coffee.

About This Blog#

This blog was first created on June 29, 2023, originally intended as a portfolio for job hunting. Looking back, my limited experience and mindset at that time made it difficult to produce quality content, and that job search was unlikely to succeed. However, there were some decent posts like Processing Raw Images in MATLAB, though most were hidden as drafts.

Later, a fortunate opportunity ended that strange job search period, and I lost motivation to update the blog, occasionally using it for odd purposes. For example, I once shared postcard stories with a French couple I met at an Airbnb in Paris.

This was the story of the blog’s initial phase. Some of the old content can still be found in the Archive üåÉ.

Being a static site deployed on GitHub Pages, other GitHub Pages under my account share the same domain. Visitors accessing just the domain would see this outdated, messy blog.

To better share project pages and utilize Cloudflare’s Tunnel feature, I purchased the domain jackchou00.icu, making the abandoned blog issue more apparent.

On January 20, 2025, to satisfy my desire to share and a bit of vanity‚Äîand to ensure the domain doesn’t point to a neglected blog‚Äîthe revamped JacksBlog was officially launched. This time, its purpose is to document research and interests, and share photos and life experiences.

The site is generated by Hugo using a slightly modified PaperMod theme, deployed on GitHub Pages with Cloudflare DNS and proxy.
